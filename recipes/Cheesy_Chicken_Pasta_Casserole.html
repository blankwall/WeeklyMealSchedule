Cheesy Chicken Pasta Casserole

Creamy pasta bake with tender chicken, mixed vegetables, and a golden cheese topping. Kid-friendly and packed with hidden nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. penne pasta
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 can (10.5 oz.) cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the cooked pasta, shredded chicken, cream of mushroom soup, milk, and frozen vegetables.
  4. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and top with the shredded cheddar cheese.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Serve hot, with a side of roasted broccoli with parmesan sprinkle and garlic bread.

Side and Protein

Serve this cheesy chicken pasta casserole with a side of roasted broccoli with a parmesan sprinkle and garlic bread. The main proteins in this dish are chicken and cheese.
