Breakfast for Dinner: Pancake & Egg Extravaganza

Fluffy buttermilk pancakes with customizable toppings, scrambled eggs, and crispy bacon. Interactive meal where kids can design their own plates.

Ingredients

  • Buttermilk pancake mix
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Maple syrup
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate chips
  • Sprinkles

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pancake batter according to the package instructions.
  2. Cook the bacon until crispy, then drain on paper towels.
  3. Scramble the eggs in a skillet with a little butter.
  4. Cook the pancakes on a griddle or in a skillet, flipping when bubbles appear on the surface.
  5. Arrange the pancakes, eggs, and bacon on plates.
  6. Invite the kids to customize their plates with their favorite toppings from the provided options.

Side Dish and Protein

Side Dish

Fresh fruit salad and hash browns

Protein

Eggs, Bacon
