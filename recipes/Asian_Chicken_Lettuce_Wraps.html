Asian Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Teriyaki-glazed ground chicken served with crisp lettuce leaves, rice, and an array of fun toppings like crispy wontons and green onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 head of lettuce, leaves separated
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup crispy wontons
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground chicken over medium heat until browned and cooked through, 5-7 minutes.
  2. Stir in the teriyaki sauce and let the mixture simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Serve the teriyaki chicken in the lettuce leaves, accompanied by the cooked rice, crispy wontons, and sliced green onions.

Side Dish

Vegetable Fried Rice

Protein

Ground Chicken
