Honey Mustard Pot Roast

Tender slow-cooked beef roast with a sweet and tangy honey mustard glaze. Served with roasted seasonal vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 angus beef bottom round roast
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Assorted seasonal vegetables (e.g., potatoes, carrots, onions)

Instructions

  1. Place the beef roast in a slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, and onion powder. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour the honey mustard mixture over the beef roast, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Add the assorted seasonal vegetables around the roast.
  5. Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  6. Remove the roast and vegetables from the slow cooker. Shred the beef and serve with the roasted vegetables.

Side Dish

Creamy mashed potatoes and roasted carrots

Protein

Beef
