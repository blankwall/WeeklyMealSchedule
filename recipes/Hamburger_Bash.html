Hamburger Bash

A fun and family-friendly meal featuring juicy hamburgers and crispy tater tots.

Ingredients

  • Frozen hamburger patties
  • Frozen tater tots
  • Hamburger buns

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the packages of your frozen hamburger patties and tater tots.
  2. Arrange the frozen hamburger patties and tater tots on separate baking sheets.
  3. Bake the hamburger patties and tater tots according to the package instructions, flipping halfway through the cooking time.
  4. Once the hamburger patties and tater tots are cooked, assemble your Hamburger Bash by placing a hamburger patty on each bun and topping with your favorite condiments.
  5. Serve the crispy tater tots alongside the juicy hamburgers for a delicious and satisfying meal!
