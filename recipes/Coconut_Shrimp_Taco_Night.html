Coconut Shrimp Taco Night

Crispy coconut-crusted shrimp in soft tortillas with a mild mango salsa. Colorful, crunchy, and fun to assemble.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 mango, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the shredded coconut and flour. In another shallow bowl, beat the eggs.
  3. Dip the shrimp into the egg mixture, then coat with the coconut-flour mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  4. Arrange the coated shrimp on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the shrimp are golden brown and crispy.
  6. In a small bowl, mix together the mango, red onion, jalapeño, lime juice, and cilantro. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve the coconut shrimp in the tortillas, topped with the mango salsa.

Sides and Protein

Serve the Coconut Shrimp Tacos with a side of black beans and cilantro-lime rice. The main protein is shrimp.
